Welcome to Nx Furniture

Nx design furniture offers a variety of unique and functional designs for your home. From coffee tables to beds, can furniture provides the perfect pieces to make your space feel more like home!

Nx design and handicraft furniture is a company that designs and manufactures furniture made of varity of color full ropes & cans. The can furniture is made of 100% original material and is designed to be both affordable and durable.

we're a team of designers and craftsmen on a mission to design furniture that is both functional and beautiful. we believe that good design should be accessible to everyone, so we've made our products affordable without compromising on quality.
